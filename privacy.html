Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 June 2025  ·  Last updated: 12 June 2026

Onedays Plumbing Services ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly, in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (POPIA) and other applicable South African law.

1. What Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact us for a quote, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Suburb / address
  • Service request details (type of plumbing, electrical, or painting issue)
  • Urgency level selected on our quote form

We only collect information that is necessary to respond to your enquiry and provide our services.

2. Why We Collect Your Information

Your personal information is collected to:

  • Respond to your service or quote request
  • Schedule and confirm appointments
  • Communicate with you about your booking or job
  • Follow up after job completion to ensure satisfaction

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing purposes. We will not contact you for unrelated services without your consent.

3. How We Store and Process Your Information

Quote requests submitted via the online form on our website are processed through Netlify Forms, a third-party form handling service. Form submissions are stored securely and accessible only to authorised Onedays team members.

Your information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties, except where strictly necessary to provide the requested service (for example, where a specialist subcontractor is required and you have been informed in advance).

4. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and job records for a reasonable period (generally up to 3 years) for operational, warranty, and bookkeeping purposes. After this period, personal data is securely deleted or anonymised. You may request deletion earlier — see your rights below.

5. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention obligations)
  • Object to the processing of your information in certain circumstances
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa if you believe your rights have been violated

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: info@onedaysplumbing.co.za

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site — including pages visited, session duration, and traffic sources. This helps us improve the site and our services.

Google Analytics sets cookies in your browser to collect anonymised usage data. We do not use this data to identify you personally. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by:

  • Adjusting your browser's cookie settings
  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (e.g. Google Maps, WhatsApp). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Yes. Every Onedays plumber is PIRB (Plumbing Industry Registration Board) accredited, fully insured, and carries up-to-date certifications. We do not use unverified subcontractors — when you book Onedays, you get Onedays.

Yes — in addition to our core plumbing services, Onedays also offers qualified electrical repairs and installations (geyser element wiring, plug points, light fittings, DB board inspections, CoC issuing) and professional painting services including interior, exterior, waterproofing, and post-repair wall patching.

Yes. We issue Certificates of Compliance (CoC) for all qualifying geyser replacements and electrical installations. This is a legal requirement in South Africa and we handle the paperwork on-site so you're fully covered.
